    The hotel

    Eurostars Gran Hotel Lugo

    The elegant Eurostars Gran Hotel Santiago, just a stone's throw from the University of Santiago International Campus of Excellence, is the perfect hotel for an unforgettable visit to the capital of Galicia.

    Just minutes from the train station and historical city centre, with its charming cobblestone streets and famously imposing cathedral, Eurostars Gran Hotel Santiago offers guests a wide range of quality services in an unbeatable setting, taking great care in all the details.

    The hotel's 143 rooms have been designed with special care in the decoration, creating elegant spaces equipped with a wide range of the best services. Also fully equipped, the hotel's 11 large, elegant events rooms are perfect for making sure any event is a success.

    Facilities

    Gastronomy

    Gran Café, located in Eurostars Gran Hotel Lugo, is a tribute to Galician gastronomy and the typical products of the region. The local is a combination of a cafeteria and a restaurant, thus offering the possibility to have breakfast, snack, have dinner or enjoy a drink, within the facilities of the hotel.

    The sophisticated and contemporary décor creates the perfect atmosphere to enjoy a flavourful break anytime during the day.

    Salones

    The Eurostars Gran Hotel Lugo 4* includes 7 meeting rooms for holding meetings or events of a personal nature. For improved comfort, each room has different capacities and arrangements

    Likewise, for holding conferences, we offer a reception area, noticeboards, projectors, videoconference availability, Wi-Fi, photocopier, screens and sound system, among other services. Coffee break, cocktail and business meal services are also available.

    Contact us at eventos@eurostarsgranhotellugo.com.
